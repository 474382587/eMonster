+- Editor's Notes
Johanna Ortiz's Resort '19 moodboard includes 19th century paintings of Spanish flamenco dancers by British artist George Owen Wynne Apperley. Beautifully made from rich jade silk, this 'Contigo En La Distancia' maxi dress is embroidered with flowers and gathered at the front to create an elegantly draped silhouette. The crocheted fringing along the hem is taken from traditional scarves that have been crafted in the south of Spain for centuries. Match your accessories to the golden threads.
